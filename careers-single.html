JOB DESCRIPTION

Call customer for give the information about the product.

2. Marketing the product to customer.

3. Meet with customer point,

4. Collect the necessary document which needed for gives Credit Card and Personal Loan for a customer.

5. Fill up the customer form for loan and credit card.

6. Send the CC and PL form to head office and submit the document.

7. Follow up the document by document tracker and with the customer.

8. Regularly follow up with existing customer and try to solve their problem in collaboration with Head Office.

9. To collaborate with marketing team and help to develop innovative programs that drive demand.

10. Ability to grasp and present our product’s value proposition in a way that resonates and clearly articulates how we solve our customers’ problems.

11. Regularly collaborate with product managers and sales channels.

12. Collaborate with product management and marketing communications to develop product positioning and messaging that resonate with our target buyer personas.

13. Give information to develop a marketing plan for the products you support in conjunction with our marketing team, including key activities and budgets to support the retention of existing customers and the acquisition of new customers.

14. Assess the effectiveness of the marketing programs that support your products on an ongoing basis, and report back to the business on required changes.

15. Work and give data to manager for launches of net-new products and releases of existing products, and help manager to manage the cross-functional implementation of the plan.

16. Act as the primary thought leader for the products you support externally, including speaking engagements and written works.

17. Give support to customer; guide them on the problems.
